Customer 360 Playbook

How to interpret account relationship signals:

1. Account plus open opportunity:
This usually indicates active revenue motion. Mention the opportunity stage, owner, and any commercial blockers if available.

2. Account plus open case:
This indicates delivery or support risk. If an open case exists alongside an active opportunity, call out that the commercial motion may be at risk.

3. Account plus contacts:
Use contacts to identify stakeholders, executive sponsors, and business owners. Contacts help explain who matters in the relationship, not just what records exist.

4. Account plus campaign influence:
Use campaign relationships to explain pipeline context and how demand generation may support open revenue opportunities.

5. Owner relationships:
If a user owns an opportunity or case, mention the accountable person in summaries so the answer feels operational, not just descriptive.
